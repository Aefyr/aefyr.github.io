SAI (Split APKs Installer) Privacy Policy
Overview
SAI (Split APKs Installer) ("app") uses AppGallery Connect Crash ("Crash") and AppGallery Connect Analytics Kit ("Analytics Kit") services to collect app errors logs and gather some analytics data.

By using app, you agree with this privacy policy as well as with Crash's and Analytics Kit's privacy policies.
Please note, that SAI developer is not responsible for the actions of apps you install with SAI
What data is collected by developer?
Crash provides developer with data stated in their privacy policy

Analytics Kit is used to collect standard metrics which you can find in it's documentation
What data is collected by Crash and how is it used?
Refer to Crash's privacy policy
What data is collected by Analytics Kit and how is it used?
Refer to Analytics Kit's privacy policy
How is data used by developer?
Developer uses data exclusively to analyze app errors and keep track of app usage stats
Who is data shared with?
Developer doesn't share the data
How is data protected?
Developer doesn't store the data, services that provide it do and it's protected as stated in their privacy policies. Developer follows best security practices when accessing the data
Where is data stored?
Crash and Analytics Kit store data on their servers in Russia
